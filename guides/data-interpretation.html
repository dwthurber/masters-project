Data Interpretation

Interpretation is good ideas, useful themes, convincing explanations, and ties of your data to literature. It should emphasize Integration of data summary and the literature findings. Data interpretation should include:

  • Integration of your data results summary and the literature
  • Interpretions (explain the meaning of the data)
  • New models or interventions suggested by your research
  • Limitations of the study
  • Changes to consider for next time/ Lessons learned
  • A brief conclusion that leads to the conclusion section of the MP
